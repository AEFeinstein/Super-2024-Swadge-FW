# Camp Idle game (Pending theme)

You play as a person in the forest that has to scrounge around for things to build a camp to survive the night. Monsters come out at night and the player has to prepare for each wave.

Resources are foraged when the app isn't open, and once the player connects, the items are collected and can be used.

Night happens after a certain amount of time (30 IRL minutes of game time) or when the player chooses to enter a new night. One a night has started, the player has to defend themselves with both active and passive defenses. If the player survives, the day advances. If they fail, it resets to the previous day.

The player's health and energy are determined by how well their living situation is. If they're warmer, dry, not tired, etc they've got more energy to fight at night.

There's a a scoring system. Each monster is worth a flat amount of points, but there's a multiplier based on the night. Using ***FLOATS***. I'm a monster.
